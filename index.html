About

Social experiment ?

Altruism Token is an empty token which purpose is only to offer you differents ways to use your contribution

The idea came out after watching how people react to the Useless Token. Instead of doing another empty token, as we see a lot this last days, why not use the blockchain for a new kind of contract.

Differents ways ?

Yes. Based on your contribution amount, differents scenarios are available. It's up to you to choose how you want to contribute..

Check below, but you'll have 3 modes available related to your contribution.

Details

Token distribution

The formula is very complex and required a high knowledge in mathematics. For gifted people, here's the formula :
1 ETH = 1 000 ALTR

Normal mode is enabled if your contribution is between 0.01 and 0.03 ETH.
Altruism mode is enabled if your contribution is at least of 0.03 ETH.
Hacked mode is enabled if your contribution equals 0.04 ETH.

Normal mode

Normal mode is the poor functionnality of the contract. It is activated for each transaction when the value of ETH sent is at least 0.01 and less than of 0.03.
The mode work like this:

  • 100% of the value is kept for me, myself, and I.
  • You got the tokens you contribute for.
Altruism mode

Altruism mode is the main functionnality of the contract. It is activated for each transaction when the value of ETH sent is at least of 0.03.
The mode work like this:

  • 66% of the value is kept for me, myself, and I.
  • 33% is sent to a previous contributor, randomly selected.
  • You got the tokens you contribute for.
Hacked mode

Hacked mode is used to overrule the altruism mode at a specific amount. It is activated for each transaction when the value of ETH sent is equal to 0.04.
The mode work like this:

  • 100% of the value is kept for me, myself, and I.
  • You got 800 tokens! Yes. As much as if you contributed for 0.8 ETH.
